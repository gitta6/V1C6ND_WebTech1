Frequently Asked Questions

Here you can find answers to some of the most common questions about our bath bomb business. If you have any other questions, feel free to contact us!


1. What are handmade bath bombs?


Handmade bath bombs are spherical or shaped bath products crafted with a combination of natural ingredients like baking soda, citric acid, Epsom salts, essential oils, and other skin-friendly components. They fizz when placed in water, releasing fragrances, colors, and moisturizing agents to enhance your bathing experience.

2. How do I use a handmade bath bomb?


Using a handmade bath bomb is simple. Fill your bathtub with warm water and drop the bath bomb gently into the water. It will start to fizz, releasing its scents, colors, and nourishing elements. You can sit back, relax, and enjoy your soothing, aromatic bath.

3. Are handmade bath bombs safe for my skin?


Generally, handmade bath bombs are safe for your skin, as they often contain natural and skin-friendly ingredients. However, it's essential to check the product's ingredient list for any specific sensitivities or allergies you may have. If you have sensitive skin, it's a good idea to do a patch test before using a new bath bomb.

4. How should I store handmade bath bombs?


To maintain their quality, store handmade bath bombs in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and moisture. Airtight containers or sealed bags can also help keep them fresh and ready for your next bath.

5. Can handmade bath bombs help with relaxation and stress relief?


Yes, our handmade bath bombs are formulated with essential oils and other calming ingredients that can help with relaxation and stress relief. The aromatherapy and soothing properties of these bath bombs can provide a tranquil and refreshing bath experience.
